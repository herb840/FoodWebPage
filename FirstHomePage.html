Why I Love Food

I love food because of the enless possibilities there are to creating dishes. One little seasoning can change the dish completely, bringing it to a new taste level. Foods I enjoy most are mexican dishes, pizzas, BBQ's, and Pastas. A great way to enhance the taste of your food is to know when to add the seasonings. A helpful tip is to add salt and pepper after the eggs have cooked, instead of seasoning beforehand.
